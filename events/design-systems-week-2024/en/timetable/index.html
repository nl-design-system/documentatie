Timetable Design Systems Week 2024

From 2 to 5 October 2024 NL Design System organised the fourth edition of Design Systems Week. Speakers from various organisations joined us for short talks about the how and why of design systems. In 2024, there were talks both in Dutch and English. For convenience, you can view a program of all talks that were in English.

This page has timeslots with talks in English, while the Dutch timetable shows the complete Design Systems Week 2024 timetable.
